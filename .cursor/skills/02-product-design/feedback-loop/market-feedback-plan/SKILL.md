# Market Feedback Plan

## When to Use

- Product strategy includes market signals
- Need to monitor reviews, competitive, support
- Planning feedback collection cadence

## Process

1. **Sources** — App store reviews, G2/Capterra, support tickets, social.
2. **Cadence** — How often to collect, who owns it.
3. **Handover** — To PM, Discovery (competitive analysis refresh).

## Output

- Market feedback sources
- Collection cadence
- Handover to PM / Discovery

## Handover Template

Use [handover-template.md](handover-template.md) for handover format.

## Downstream Use

- Informs Discovery competitive analysis cadence
- Informs PM backlog prioritization
- No wireframes (operational, not in-app)
